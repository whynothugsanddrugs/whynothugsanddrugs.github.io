Is there someone you’re feeling politically divided from that you want to reach out to? Imagine that this person receives a package in the mail. It contains 15 sealed envelopes inside, to open one at a time or all at once. It’s a gift from you, made with care.

Artists from around the nation have created and shared their reactions, encouragement, perspectives, and hopes through their artworks, for you to send . All the art pieces have been lovingly created with the intent to share and connect with people who hold different perspectives. You can preview the artworks that will be included in the package, below. You can include your perspective as a letter or artwork, if you’d like, by completing the survey at the bottom of the page.

Like a box of chocolates, the pieces are unique, beautiful, and interesting – each piece a surprise for the receiver of the package. They may love some pieces; they may not completely agree with some of the pieces. Some pieces are organically political. As with many conversations these days, I’m not sure how each piece will be received by the person who’s opening the packages but I believe that art has the potential to start new dialogues, reflections, and something creative on both ends.

"We have a lot more in common with people despite the noise around us that tells us otherwise. Turn to nature and you'll witness the evidence of our shared humanity. We all feel the same way when we're at the bottom of a towering redwood, when we feel the warmth of a sunrise across a vast ocean, or when we breath in the air after a refreshing thunderstorm. Communing with nature restores us and makes us whole again. Communing with nature is something we can all benefit from. It creates space in us to grow love, patience, and awareness. We are all blessed to live on this planet together to witness the majesty of Mother Earth. We have a lot more in common than we think. Lets go back to nature together and remember our humanity."
